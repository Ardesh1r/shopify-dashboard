### Metrics to Evaluate
- [ ] GitHub stars (target: 500+)

- [ ] Chrome extension installs (target: 5,000+)
- [ ] Paid users (target: 100+)

- [ ] Email subscribers (target: 500+)

### Go/No-Go Decision
**Build SaaS if:**
- ✅ 500+ GitHub stars
- ✅ 100+ paid Chrome extension users
- ✅ Consistent feature requests for backend features
- ✅ Competitors are raising funding/getting traction
- ✅ You have 6+ months runway

**Stay consulting-focused if:**
- ❌ Low organic traction (<200 stars)
- ❌ Consulting is generating $15k+/mo
- ❌ You prefer services over product
- ❌ Market feedback is lukewarm
